About

I'm a Physics MSc candidate at ETH Zurich (GPA 5.63/6.0), specialising in machine learning and probabilistic modeling. My master's thesis — graded 6.0/6.0 — focused on implementing gauge-equivariant U-Net architectures for diffusion models and continuous normalizing flows, as part of an international research collaboration in computational physics.

Beyond research, I've won 1st place at the UN OCHA Datathon and the UBS Case Competition at ETH Zurich, and I serve as Head of Marketing at the Analytics Club at ETH (ACE). I enjoy problems that sit at the edge of rigorous theory and practical impact.
